Our Story

Founded in 2000s by master craftsman Sara Sinha in the heart of India, our brand began with a simple vision: to create timepieces that would stand the test of time.

What started as a small workshop has grown into a globally recognized brand, yet we've never lost sight of our core values: precision, quality, and timeless design.

Today, each Imperion Watches is still meticulously crafted by hand, combining traditional techniques with cutting-edge technology to create timepieces that are both beautiful and reliable.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our timepieces and services.

What is the warranty period for Imperion watches?

+
All Imperion watches come with a comprehensive 5-year international warranty covering manufacturing defects and movement issues.

Do you offer custom engraving?

+
Yes, we provide complimentary custom engraving services for all watch purchases. Our master engravers can personalize your timepiece with names, dates, or special messages.

How often should I service my watch?

+
We recommend servicing your Imperion Watches every 3-5 years to maintain optimal performance and preserve its longevity.

Do you offer international shipping?

+
Yes, we offer worldwide shipping with full insurance and tracking. Delivery times vary by location, typically 3-7 business days for most destinations.
